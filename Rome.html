ROME
Rome has more fountains than any other city on the planet. The Pantheon is the only ancient building in pristine condition. A heavenly lightshow takes place at the Pantheon on Rome's birthday. Cats have special rights. It's technically the capital of 2 countries. The Spanish Steps aren't Spanish 


HOW TO GET THERE?
Fly: The quickest way is to take a direct flight from Dubai International Airport (DXB) to Rome Fiumicino Airport (FCO). The flight duration is approximately 10 hours and 3 minutes. Ticket prices range from €180 to €349;


WHEN TO GO THERE?
Spring (Mid-March to May) Fall (Mid-September to November) Summer (June to September) Winter (October to April):


THINGS TO DO


St. Peter’s Square is an iconic gathering place in front of St. Peter’s Basilica Palazzo Madama: This historic palace, also known as the Senate of the Italian Republic, is a blend of medieval and Renaissance architecture Colosseum and Roman Forum: Step back in time at the Colosseum, an ancient amphitheater where gladiators once battled Trevi Fountain: Toss a coin into the Trevi Fountain to ensure your return to Rome Vatican Museums and Sistine Chapel: Immerse yourself in art and history by visiting the Vatican Museums
