MADRID
Madrid was created around the year of 860 A.C It has been the capital of Spain since the 17th century According to FIFA, Real Madrid FC is the world's most successful football club of the 20th Century. Madrid is one of the greenest cities in Europe.


HOW TO GET THERE?
Fly from Dubai (DXB) to Madrid (MAD): This is the quickest option, taking approximately 11 hours and 29 minutes. Airlines such as Emirates, Qatar Airways, and Turkish Airlines operate flights on this route. The cost ranges from €185 to €457


WHEN TO GO THERE?
Fall (September to November) Winter (December to February) Spring (March to May) Summer (June to August)


THINGS TO DO
Museo del Prado: Immerse yourself in art and culture at the Prado Museum. Marvel at masterpieces by renowned artists like Velázquez, Goya, and El Greco. The museum is located at Paseo del Prado, 8, Madrid, 280141. Royal Palace of Madrid (Palacio Real): Visit the Royal Palace, an architectural gem with opulent interiors and beautiful gardens. It’s a glimpse into Spain’s royal history. You’ll find it at Calle Milaneses, 6, Madrid, 280132. Retiro Park (Parque del Retiro): Stroll through this expansive park, rent a rowboat on the lake, and admire the Crystal Palace. It’s a serene oasis in the heart of the city. Puerta del Sol: The bustling central square of Madrid, known for its iconic clock tower and the famous “Kilometer Zero” plaque.
