Macau
Macau got its name from a misunderstanding. ... It's the second richest territory in the world. It's the most densely populated region on earth


HOW TO GET THERE?
Emirates offers direct flights from Dubai International Airport (DXB) to Macau International Airport (MFM). These flights take approximately 11 hours and 58 minutes, including a stopover in Hong Kong. The total travel time may vary depending on the layover duration


WHEN TO GO THERE?
Autumn (October to December) Winter (December to February)

THINGS TO DO



Ruins of St. Paul’s (Ruínas de São Paulo): Description: Explore the iconic Ruins of St. Paul’s, the remains of a 16th-century church. Climb the steps to enjoy panoramic views of the city and learn about Macau’s history Senado Square (Largo do Senado): Description: Stroll through the historic Senado Square, a UNESCO World Heritage Site A-Ma Temple (Templo de A-Má): Description: Visit the A-Ma Temple, one of Macau’s oldest and most revered temples
