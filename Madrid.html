ROME 
Rome has more fountains than any other city on the planet. The Pantheon is the only ancient building in pristine condition. A heavenly lightshow takes place at the Pantheon on Rome's birthday. Cats have special rights. It's technically the capital of 2 countries. The Spanish Steps aren't Spanish 


HOW TO GET THERE?
Emirates offers direct flights from Dubai International Airport (DXB) to Madrid Barajas Airport (MAD). These flights take approximately 4 hours and 30 minutes and arrive at Terminal 3 in Madrid 


WHEN TO GO THERE?
Spring (March to May) Fall (September to November) Winter (December to February)      


THINGS TO DO
Prado Museum: Immerse yourself in European art, from Velázquez to Goya. The museum houses an impressive collection of masterpiecesby Velázquez, Goya, and Titian. The museum’s collection spans centuries and offers a glimpse into Spain’s rich artistic heritage. Royal Palace Boutique: Description: Discover the opulence of the Royal Palace of Madrid (Palacio Real de Madrid). Explore its lavish rooms, the Armory, and the Royal Pharmacy. Don’t miss the stunning views of the city from the palace gardens
