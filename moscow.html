Moscow
Lenin’s Preserved Body: Vladimir Lenin, the leader of the Bolshevik Revolution, passed away in 1924. His body is preserved in a mausoleum on Red Square in Moscow Yuri Gagarin’s Legacy: Yuri Gagarin, the first human to journey into space, is buried at the Kremlin Wall in Moscow Diverse Population: Surprisingly, only around 2 percent of Moscow’s population can trace their ancestry directly to the city 


HOW TO GET THERE?
Emirates offers direct flights from Dubai International Airport (DXB) to Moscow Domodedovo Airport (DME). These flights take approximately 4 hours and 30 minutes and arrive at Terminal 3 in Moscow 


WHEN TO GO THERE?
Summer (June-August) Fall (September-October) Winter (November-March) Spring (April-May) 


THINGS TO DO


Red Square (Красная площадь): Description: Red Square is a historic city square in Moscow, Russia. The buildings surrounding the square are all significant Saint Basil’s Cathedral (Собор Василия Блаженного): Description: Built by Ivan the Terrible in the 1550s, this intriguing cathedral bordering Red Square consists of nine separate chapels, each capped with its own individually shaped and colored dome State Tretyakov Gallery: Description: The State Tretyakov Gallery is the national treasury of Russian fine art. It contains more than 180,000 works of painting, sculpture, and graphics created by generations of Russian artists
